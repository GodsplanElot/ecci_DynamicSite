Nurturing Young Hearts: Covenant Children - Where Faith and Fun Collide

Hey there, parents and guardians! 🙃



Are you looking for a place where your children can grow, learn, and thrive in their faith journey? Look no further than Covenant Children, the vibrant children's department at our church. Here at Covenant Children, we're passionate about nurturing young hearts and helping kids develop a strong foundation of faith that will last a lifetime.

Ready to Get Involved? 🤗


If you're ready to enroll your child in Covenant Children or if you'd like to learn more about our programs and events, we'd love to hear from you! Reach out to us today and let's start this journey of faith together.
With love and blessings, ❤

A Word from the Head Teacher

"As the head teacher of Covenant Children, 🧐 I have the privilege of witnessing firsthand the incredible impact that our ministry has on the lives of young children. There's nothing quite like seeing a child's eyes light up as they discover God's love for the first time or hearing them recite a Bible verse they've learned in class. It's truly a blessing to be able to play a part in shaping the hearts and minds of the next generation. As parents, you have the incredible opportunity to instill in your children a love for God and His Word from a young age. It's a journey that may have its challenges, but I can assure you that it's one of the most rewarding experiences you'll ever have. So let's continue to partner together in raising children who are rooted and grounded in the love of Christ. Together, we can make a difference that will last for eternity."

Mrs Boma Tito

Head Teacher

Why Choose Covenant Children?

  • Biblical Teaching 📖: Our dedicated team of teachers is committed to providing children with a solid understanding of God's Word. Through engaging lessons, interactive activities, and age-appropriate curriculum, we make learning about the Bible fun and exciting for kids of all ages.

  • Safe and Secure Environment 🛡: We understand that safety is a top priority for parents, which is why we go above and beyond to create a safe and secure environment for your children. From background checks for all volunteers to strict check-in procedures, you can have peace of mind knowing that your kids are in good hands.

  • Fun and Fellowship 🏐: At Covenant Children, we believe that faith and fun go hand in hand. That's why we offer a variety of activities and events throughout the year that allow kids to connect with each other, build friendships, and grow in their relationship with God. From game nights to movie screenings to special holiday celebrations, there's always something exciting happening at Covenant Children!

  • Parental Involvement ⛪;: We believe that parents play a crucial role in their children's spiritual development, which is why we encourage parental involvement in our programs and activities. Whether it's volunteering in the classroom, participating in family devotions, or attending parent-child events, we're here to support and equip you as you raise your children in the way of the Lord
