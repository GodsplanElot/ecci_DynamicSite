About us

Our Mandate

“But as many as received Him, to them he gave the power to become the sons of God, even to them that believe on His name”. – John 1:12 Our church ministry as its name implies, is more than just a name but an invitation to a higher life; a call to the discovery and fulfillment of divine purpose. This was revealed in August 2011 when the mandate was delivered. The church was commissioned in October 2011 with a charge to restore men to God, leading them to fulfill purpose as they have been originally empowered.

ARTICLE 2:2
Our mandate to propagate the message of spiritual restoration and empowerment. Church planting in one way to actual this mandate and so on the 3rd of November 2021, Grace sanctuary a daughter church was opened in Abuja with many more branches on the way.

- The church mandate is carried out through an in-depth teaching of the word and a strict adherence to covenant principles

- The mandate also includes providing spiritual guidance and knowledge to ensure spiritual growth in a constantly evolving environment

- ECCI operates in the 5 (Five) purposes of the universal church through the :

o Membership Team: Establish care and love through fellowship
o Maturity Team: Grow deeper by connecting through discipleship
o Ministry Team: Expand when we communicate our gifts through ministry
o Evangelism & Baptism Team: Fulfill the call, multiply through evangelism and baptism.
o Worship Team: Stronger through worship

Our Services

3.3.3 TYPES OF SERVICES
To ensure that the general aims of the local assemblies are met several types of services exist through which these aims and the mandate of restoration given to Empowered can be maximized.
a. Regular Services
b. Special Services
REGULAR SERVICES HOLD EVERY WEEK AND INCLUDE
Sunday Service(s) – Sundays
Weekly Prayer Service – Thursday
Friends and Family Fellowship – Holding in approved centers, in members houses

SPECIAL SERVICES
Monthly Communion Services – 1st Sunday of every month
Monthly Family Services – Last Sunday of every month
Thanksgiving\Dedication, Marriage blessing etc. will hold on the last Sunday of the month as they arise
“IN HIS PRESENCE” – January every year
PREVAILING PRAYERS – September every year
SENIORS PASTORS BIRTHDAY – 1ST OF MARCH
ENCOUNTER CONFERENCE – OCTOBER
Where there is a need for other services to be held there will be a formal notification and approval granted and such services will not take precedence over the daily established services.
