Join Us for an Unforgettable Radiant Teens Camp 2024: A Journey of Faith, Fun, and Fellowship

Are you ready for an exhilarating adventure filled with faith, fun, and fellowship? Well, get excited because this year's Radiant Teens Camp 2024 is just around the corner, and it's going to be an event like no other! As part of Empowered Christian Church International, we're thrilled to invite each and every one of you to join us for an unforgettable experience.

Deepen Your Faith: Radiant Teens Camp isn't just about having fun (although there'll be plenty of that!). It's also a time to dive deeper into your faith journey. Through inspiring worship sessions, powerful messages, and engaging discussions, you'll have the opportunity to grow spiritually and strengthen your relationship with God

As you participate in these deep experiences and connect with God in a meaningful way, may you be strengthened in your faith, encouraged in your journey, and empowered to live boldly for Christ in every area of your life.

Victory Nill

Radiant Teens Pastor

As you prepare to embark on this year's Radiant Teens Camp, get ready for an incredible opportunity to deepen your connection with God in ways you never imagined. Amidst the laughter, games, and camaraderie, there lies a profound journey awaiting you – a journey of spiritual growth, discovery, and transformation.

Encounter God in Nature: As you immerse yourself in the beauty of God's creation during outdoor activities and adventures, allow yourself to experience the wonder and majesty of His handiwork. Whether you're gazing at a starry sky, marveling at a breathtaking sunset, or feeling the gentle breeze on your face, let nature serve as a reminder of God's presence and power.
